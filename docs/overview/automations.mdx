---
title: "Automations Kit"
description: "Define custom logic for automating the distribution and management of tokens."
---

Control your tokens in response to on or offchain events to automate the rules and logic of your organization.
Automations Kit templatize linking on and offchain activity to token movement, reducing effort to build
fully automated and transparent distribution mechanisms.

To evaluate your onchain automation needs, [schedule a short call](https://calendly.com/symmtry/chat) with our team.
